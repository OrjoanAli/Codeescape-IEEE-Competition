About Codescape

Codescape is IEEE Computer Society UJ's premier Matrix-inspired coding competition. Navigate through three challenging stages that will test your problem-solving skills, database knowledge, and coding speed.

Join fellow programmers in this cyberpunk adventure where only the most skilled will escape the digital maze and claim victory in the ultimate coding challenge.
